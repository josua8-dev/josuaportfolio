Hobbies & Sports

What I'm passionate about outside of coding

Basketball

The game that defines my competitive spirit.

I have been training religiously for about 3 years now. Putting in the hours necessary to get better every day. All of the late nights and early mornings, waking up at 5am and waiting until 10pm so that the gym is free. All of this commitment will one day pay off.

Inspirations
There was always something about Kobe Bryant that made him different. I know I wasn't playing while he was still alive, but by the time I first watched one of his games I fell in love with the game. His work ethic was unmatched and his will to win was something I strived to have myself.

Alongside Kobe, Kyrie Irving has been a huge inspiration for me. His style of play is so creative and unique that it always amazes me to watch him on the court.

Basketball teaches me discipline, teamwork, and the value of consistent practice.

Fitness

Building functional strength and conditioning that translates to better performance in all areas of life is the primary goal here.

Calisthenics
I am currently focusing on bodyweight based workouts to experiment with different ways to get stronger. I have realised that for me, getting stronger here translates best to other areas of my training.

The Gym is where I challenge myself and push my limits to ensure I won't stay content with what I have now.

Learning

I believe that to grow, one must never stop learning.

Whether it's exploring new programming languages, diving into tech related hobbies, such as custom Keyboards or the world of Linux Distros, or expanding my knowledge in various fields. Learning new things keeps me curious and engaged.

"Live as if you were to die tomorrow. Learn as if you were to live forever." - Mahatma Gandhi

New Challenges

Completed my first Marathon on the 2nd of November 2025.

After failing my first attempt earlier this year, I was determined to try again and succeed. There was no question that I wouln't be able to do it.

The hardest part of it all was me deciding to do this without any prior long distance training. Playing Basketball isn't really a long-distance endurance sport, so this was a new challenge for me. However it was all the more rewarding.

Running teaches me patience, resilience, and the importance of setting goals.
